Paid:

G. PAYWALL / LIMIT EXPERIENCE
Check:
- does the subtle in-chat nudge work?

H. INTERACTION RHYTHM
Check:
- does SSE still feel smooth?

I. FAILURE HONESTY

- does anything still fake success?

Determine whether TrainChat now feels like:
