PHASE 3 — EXERCISE ↔ PRODUCT INTELLIGENCE

Objective

Connect TrainChat’s exercise library to the Performance Intelligence Directory.

Currently TrainChat understands:

Goals
Physical Qualities
Training Methods
Products

TrainChat also understands:

Exercises

However, these systems are not yet connected.

Phase 3 creates the relationship layer between exercises and products so TrainChat can reason about available equipment, substitutions, regressions, progressions, and facility constraints.

This is the phase where Product Intelligence begins influencing programming decisions.
