PHASE 2 — PRODUCT INTELLIGENCE & TRAINCHAT KNOWLEDGE CONNECTIONS

Objective

Transform the Performance Intelligence Directory from a static catalog into a structured knowledge layer that TrainChat can reason about.

Phase 1 established visibility and credibility.

Phase 2 establishes relationships.

The goal is for TrainChat to understand:

Goal → Physical Quality → Training Method → Product

This phase does NOT require purchasing functionality, affiliate links, or ecommerce.

This phase focuses entirely on knowledge and reasoning.

⸻

CORE OBJECTIVE

Today the directory knows what a product is.

After Phase 2 the system should know:

Why it exists.

What adaptation it develops.

What sports benefit most.

What physical qualities it influences.

What training methods it supports.

PHASE 2 SUCCESS CRITERIA

A visitor should immediately understand:

TrainChat is not simply a workout generator.

TrainChat understands performance tools, training methods, and the relationships between them.

Internally, TrainChat should now have enough structured information to begin recommending products and methods based on goals in future phases.
