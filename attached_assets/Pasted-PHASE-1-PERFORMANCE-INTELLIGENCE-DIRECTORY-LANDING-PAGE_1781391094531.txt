PHASE 1 — PERFORMANCE INTELLIGENCE DIRECTORY (LANDING PAGE)

Objective

Create a new section at the bottom of the TrainChat landing page called “Performance Intelligence Directory.”

The purpose of this section is not e-commerce or product sales.

The purpose is to demonstrate the depth of knowledge available inside TrainChat and reinforce that TrainChat is a specialized performance intelligence platform built for coaches, athletes, and performance professionals.

No AI functionality is required for Phase 1.

No recommendation engine is required for Phase 1.

This is a display and credibility layer only.
