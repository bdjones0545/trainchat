TASK 1 — UPGRADE THE EMPTY-STATE COPY
==================================================

Replace the current generic empty-state copy with stronger language.

Current headline:
- “What do you want to build?”

Upgrade to something stronger such as:
- “Build your training system”
OR
- “Let’s build your next phase”
