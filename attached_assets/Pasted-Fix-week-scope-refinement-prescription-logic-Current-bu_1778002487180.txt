Fix week-scope refinement prescription logic.

Current bugs:
1. “More Endurance” change log says endurance focus was applied, but exercises still show 3–6 reps and 30–60 sec rest. The visible prescription does not match endurance.
2. “More Strength” incorrectly applies strength prescriptions to every exercise, including core, trunk, mobility, auxiliary, and accessory work.

Required behavior:
Week-scope refinements must apply prescriptions by exercise role/category, not globally.

Add or update a helper:

getPrescriptionForExerciseTransformation(exercise, transformation, sessionContext)

It should inspect:
- exercise.role
- exercise.category
- exercise.movementPattern
- exercise.name
- isPrimaryLift
- isPowerExercise
- isCoreOrTrunk
- isAccessoryOrAuxiliary
- isMobilityOrPrep

Rules:

For strength_focus:
- Primary compound lifts: 3–5 sets, 3–6 reps, 2–3 min rest
- Secondary compound lifts: 2–4 sets, 5–8 reps, 90–150 sec rest
- Accessories/auxiliary: keep moderate reps, usually 8–15 reps
- Core/trunk: keep time/quality or 8–15+ reps; do NOT force 3–6 reps
- Mobility/prep: do not change to strength reps

For conditioning_focus / endurance:
- Primary lifts: moderate load/density, 2–4 sets, 8–12 reps, 45–90 sec rest
- Accessories: 2–3 sets, 12–20 reps, 30–60 sec rest
- Core/trunk: 2–4 sets, 20–45 sec holds OR 10–20 reps, 30–60 sec rest
- Power exercises/jumps: do not turn into high-rep sloppy jumps; use low reps with shorter density OR swap to conditioning-safe alternatives
- Rest should generally decrease compared to strength

For power/explosive:
- Power exercises: 2–5 sets, 2–5 reps, 90–180 sec rest
- Heavy primary lifts used for power: 2–4 sets, 2–5 reps, 2–3 min rest
- Accessories/core stay supportive; do not turn all accessories into power exercises

For reduce_time:
- Reduce sets first, preserve intent
- Do not blindly change reps

Also:
- Do not overwrite all exercises with one prescription.
- Preserve the original prescription when the transformation is not relevant to that exercise role.
- Update the UI-visible sets/reps/rest fields, not just hidden metadata.
- Add a verifier:
  - If transformation is endurance and most exercises still show 3–6 reps, flag/repair.
  - If transformation is strength and core/accessory exercises show 3–6 reps, flag/repair.

Acceptance tests:
1. Click “More Endurance” on a 3-day strength block.
Expected:
- Primary lifts shift toward 8–12 reps or density work.
- Accessories/core shift toward higher reps/time-based work.
- Rest is shorter.
- Visible exercise cards reflect the endurance change.

2. Click “More Strength.”
Expected:
- Primary/secondary compound lifts become strength-focused.
- Core/accessory exercises do NOT become 3–6 reps.
- Band Pallof Press, Ab Wheel, hip thrust accessories, auxiliary work, and mobility/prep retain appropriate support prescriptions.
