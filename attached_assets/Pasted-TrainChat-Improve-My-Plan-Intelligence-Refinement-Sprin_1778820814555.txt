TrainChat — Improve My Plan Intelligence Refinement Sprint
Goal: Evolve “Improve My Plan” from a static onboarding wizard into a visible athlete-intelligence calibration system deeply integrated with Atlas, Behavioral Intelligence, Forecasting, Progression Intelligence, and longitudinal memory.

PRIORITY P0 — Intelligence Visibility + Coaching Presence

T001 — Rename + Reframe the Flow
Current label:
- “Improve My Plan”

Replace with intelligence-first framing:
Primary recommendation:
- “Teach Atlas About You”

Secondary subtitle:
- “The more I understand your training, recovery, and lifestyle, the smarter your coaching becomes.”

Update all entry points:
- modal title
- CTA launch buttons
- empty states
- onboarding references
- completion confirmation copy

Goal:
This should feel like calibrating an intelligent coach, not editing settings.

---

T002 — Atlas Presence Throughout the Flow
Inject contextual coaching commentary into every onboarding step.

Pattern:
Each section gets:
- one short “Why Atlas asks this” explanation,
- dynamically tied to the current field.

Examples:

Training age:
“Your training age changes how aggressively I progress strength and volume.”

Schedule consistency:
“I use this to decide how adaptive your plan needs to be week to week.”

Recovery questions:
“This helps me predict fatigue before performance drops.”

Implementation:
- lightweight deterministic copy map
- keyed by section id
- rendered beneath section headers
- subtle visual style (muted cyan coaching tone)

Goal:
The user should feel coached during onboarding, not surveyed.

T003 — Precision Score Redesign
Current:
- completion percentage only

Replace with:
COACHING PRECISION SYSTEM

Precision now measures:
1. Context completeness
2. Behavioral stability
3. Longitudinal consistency
4. Recovery confidence
5. Adaptation certainty
6. Memory confidence

New tiers:
0–25:
“Basic Coaching”
Generic adaptations only

26–50:
“Context-Aware Coaching”
Better exercise and recovery decisions

51–75:
“Adaptive Coaching”
Atlas can personalize progression and fatigue management

76–100:
“Performance Intelligence”
Longitudinal adaptation, predictive recovery, and athlete-specific coaching

Implementation:
- new calculateCoachingPrecision()
- onboarding contributes initial baseline
- readiness/check-ins update dynamically
- behavioral consistency modifies score
- confidence-weighted memory entries contribute

Goal:
Precision becomes “coach certainty,” not form completion.

- explanation-first coaching
